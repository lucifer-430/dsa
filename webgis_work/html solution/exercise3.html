The third satellite in the series Indian Remote Sensing (IRS) satellite series was IRS-1C which was launched in December, 1995 has three sensors:

  • a single-channel panchromatic (PAN
  • a medium resolution four-channel Linear Imaging Self-scanning Sensor (LISS-III)
  • a coarse resolution two-channel Wide Field Sensor (WiFS).
